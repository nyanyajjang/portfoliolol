Comic Book Web Site Advertisement / 30 Seconds

I made this motion graphic screen for a virtual comic book advertisement.
The first scene introduces what special cartoons are shown every day.
It then quickly shows the cartoons shown by comic book web site from Monday to Saturday through the screen.
Since This is a comic book advertisement, I made it based on MARVEL site as a reference.
Also I chose one music that goes well with quick screen changes.
