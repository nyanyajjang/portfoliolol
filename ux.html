Global Job web-site

I have thought that it would be convenient and nice to have a site like this site. This is where people in various professions, beyond academic background, gender, and race, post their thoughts and lives or are interviewed and then post their writings and videos. Main will be posted for videos of the people with various occupations, and the next section will show the postings covering such people or articles written by themselves. At the bottom, there will be real-time spaces where people from different countries can find different jobs and talk about each other's jobs. The menu is also created simply by pressing a tab looking like a sticker at the top. You can easily search for a job by using search.

Also, the easiest feature here is the login feature. There are many sites where you must log in to write, but here is the place where you can write even if you don't log in. You can also easily find what you wrote later. In addition, the questions that people are wondering are being left open to ask at any time. Talented people in each profession can answer in real time quickly.
